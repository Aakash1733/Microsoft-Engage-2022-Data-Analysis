Time to dive in to the Red Ocean : The Disruptive Market Trends

The fast growth of the shared mobility market has demonstrated not just the benefits of asset-light business models, but also their capacity to scale globally. As a result, these newcomers are demonstrating that the automobile industry's capital-intensive character, which formerly acted as a barrier to entrance, is now becoming a liability.
New criteria for urban and individual mobility based on asset usage have also ushered in a slew of new players, ranging from journey planners to mobility aggregators, all of which provide consumers a single-point interface for planning, booking, and paying for all types of transportation. In short, disruption has become critical to the mobility ecosystem's rapid evolution.
The time has came for the automakers to switch to the non-conventional manufacturing areas. Explore the Non-Conventioanal Changing market trend.

Time for an Evolution in the Conventional Sector

Indian Automobile Marketplace is evolving day-by-day and the number of players in the sector increasing every year .Technologies such as bluetooth,sensors , steering tilt etc. are now available starting from the base variant . Automobiles have started merging with the IN-HOUSE technologies such as mobiles and laptops .Online tracking , Anti-theft and Over-Speed alert are just another Add-Ons. Even in this Have-It-All marketplace , technologically advanced hardwares such as A Classy Sunroof will make you stand apart in the market.
